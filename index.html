About PayIn

Tanzania's Leading Payment Service Provider

We bridge the gap between mobile operators, banks, and businesses — making digital payments simple, fast, and reliable.

Aggregating Tanzania's Payment Ecosystem

PayIn is a licensed Payment Service Provider (PSP) operating in Tanzania. We aggregate mobile money operators like M-Pesa, Yas Pesa, Airtel Money, and Halopesa alongside major banks — into a single, powerful platform.

Our mission is to empower merchants and businesses with a unified payment solution that reduces complexity, speeds up settlements, and provides full visibility into every transaction.

Why Choose PayIn?

We understand the Tanzanian payment landscape. Our platform is built from the ground up to handle the unique challenges of operating across multiple mobile money networks and banking systems in East Africa.

Whether you're a small merchant or a large enterprise, PayIn gives you the tools to accept, manage, and settle payments from any channel — all through one dashboard and one wallet.

Our Services

Everything You Need to Accept & Manage Payments

From payment aggregation to wallet management, we provide end-to-end payment solutions tailored for Tanzanian businesses.

Payment Aggregation

Collect payments from M-Pesa, Yas Pesa, Airtel Money, Halopesa, and major banks through a single integration. No need for separate contracts with each operator.

Unified Wallet System

All your collections from different operators and banks land in one wallet. View balances, track inflows, and manage payouts from a single dashboard.

Merchant Accounts

Open a merchant account in minutes. Get your own payment credentials, sub-accounts, and tools to start accepting digital payments immediately.

Management Dashboard

A powerful management system to monitor transactions, generate reports, reconcile settlements, and manage your business operations in real time.

Settlement & Payouts

Automatic daily settlements to your preferred bank account. Configure settlement schedules, split payments, and manage disbursements effortlessly.

Developer APIs

RESTful APIs with comprehensive documentation. Integrate payment collection and disbursement into your app, website, or ERP system with ease.
